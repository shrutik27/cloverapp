Word processors have always worked in straight lines – top to bottom, left to right. But that’s not how our minds work. What if we had a tool that could work in straight lines or… well… not straight lines? We think it makes for a pretty powerful thinking tool.

We built a new style of text editor from the ground up to better support creative thinking. Clover lets you explode traditional documents and work in a more free-form manner for better brainstorming, mind-mapping, and exploration.
